The Importance of the Jumpshot

“The only difference between a good shot and a bad shot is if it goes in or not.”

- Charles Barkley


Top Shooters

Basketball has risen to become one of the most popular sports in the world due to its competitive nature and unique style of play. The objective of the game is to score as many points as possible through different ways of putting the ball in the basketball hoop, simply known as the basket. The action of putting the ball in the basket is called a jump shot. These are taken from as close as three feet to the hoop to as far as twenty three feet to the hoop. Teams become more favored when they have players who have mastered this style of play and can shoot accurately and consistently from any distance to the hoop.


The following are considered some of the greatest shooters of all time:

  1. Stephen Curry
    • Considered best 3-pt shooter of all time
  2. Ray Allen
    • Predecessor to Stephen Curry and known for his impact on the Boston Celtics and the Miami Heat
  3. Reggie Miller
    • Known for his clutch performances from the 3-pt line

Here are some of the teams that have the best shooting efficiency overall in the 2025 year:

  • Houston Rockets
  • Milwaukee Bucks
  • Denver Nuggets
  • LA Lakers
  • Oklahoma City Thunder
